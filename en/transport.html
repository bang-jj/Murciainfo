Transportation

Studying abroad often involves navigating public transit, ride-sharing, or walking.

1. Tram (Tranvía)

The tram is the most convenient tool in Murcia. It has one main line (L1) covering key spots for exchange students.

💰 Fare Information

Single Ticket (Zona 1 / Zona 2)1.4€ / 1.5€
Multi-card (Bono Multitranspote)0.5€ / trip
Student Pass (Bono Campus)12€ / 28 days unlimited

* Student passes and Multi-cards must be applied for at the Tranvía office with your student ID.

⚠️ Transfer Alert:
When heading towards "Universidad," those going to UCAM must get off at Los Rectores-Terra Natura and transfer to the shuttle line for UCAM - Los Jerónimos. The same applies for the return trip (though direct trams occasionally run).

2. Bus (Autobús)

The bus system is more complex, split into City (TMurciaBus) and Suburban (TMP) systems.

  • Multi-card Fare: Each trip costs 0.8€.
  • Identification: Buses with an R on the front are typically city buses.
  • Heading to UCAM: Take bus 44 or 33.
🚨 Important Notes:
1. Always ensure the front sign says UCAM - Los Jerónimos. For example, the 44E goes toward the Foreigners' Office (Extranjería) and does NOT go to campus.
2. Buses are often late or cancelled; Google Maps data is not always 100% accurate.
