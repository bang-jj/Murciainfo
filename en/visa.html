🛂 Visa & Foreigner Identity Card (TIE)

If your stay in Spain is longer than 180 days, you are required to obtain a TIE (Tarjeta de Identidad de Extranjero) within 30 days of arrival.

Step 1: Book an Appointment (Cita Previa)

Appointments must be scheduled through the official portal:
Official Immigration Appointment Portal

Process:
1. Select Murcia as your province.
2. Select POLICIA-TOMA DE HUELLAS (Fingerprinting).
3. Enter your details and choose an available office.

💡 Advice: TIE slots are released unpredictably. Check the website every morning around 8:30 - 9:30 AM. Using a VPN to a Spanish server may improve connection stability during peak hours.

Step 2: Required Documents

  • Application Form EX-17: Completed and signed (Original + 1 Copy).
  • Valid Passport: Original plus a full photocopy of all pages.
  • Acceptance Letter: Your official enrollment document from UCAM.
  • Empadronamiento: Census certificate from Murcia City Hall.
    Office: Calle Dr. Alonso de Espejo, 7, 30007 Murcia.
  • Tax Form 790-012: Stamped proof of payment (approx. €16.08).
