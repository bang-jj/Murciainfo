🛂 Visa & Residence Card (NIE/TIE)

Upon arrival in Spain, if your stay exceeds 180 days, you must apply for a TIE (Residence Card) within one month.

Step 1: Book an Appointment (Cita Previa)

Visit the official government appointment website:
https://sede.administracionespublicas.gob.es/
Select PROCEDIMIENTOS in the top menu to show the dropdown
Click on Extranjería, then select Cita Previa de Extranjería
Scroll to the bottom and click Acceder al Procedimiento
Select Murcia as the province, and choose POLICIA-TOMA DE HUELLAS (Fingerprinting).

※ TIE appointments are extremely hard to get. It is recommended to check the website daily between 8-10 AM, and consider using a VPN to a Spanish server even before you arrive.

Step 2: Required Documents

  • Form EX-17: Original and copy.
  • Passport: Full copy (including blank pages and entry stamp page).
  • Letter of Acceptance: The official admission letter from UCAM.
  • Certificate of Census (Empadronamiento): Must be obtained at the City Hall (Ayuntamiento).
    Address: Calle Dr. Alonso de Espejo, 7, 30007 Murcia, Spain
  • Fee Tax 790-012: Paid at a bank, currently approx. €16.
